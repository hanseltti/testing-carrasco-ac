Signs That You Need Air Conditioning Repair

It’s easy to spot signs that your AC might need repair, especially if you monitor your energy bills and indoor air quality. Look out for:

  • Warm air or poor airflow, which could indicate closed vents, clogged filters, or a faulty motor
  • Water leaks, often due to a blocked condensate drain
  • Unusual noises from loose components or a malfunctioning heat pump
  • High humidity levels indoors
  • Increased energy bills
  • Unpleasant odors, suggesting mold growth or dirty filters
  • Hot spots, refrigerant pooling, or frequent cycling
