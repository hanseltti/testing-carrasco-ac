Signs That You Need Furnace Repair

Operating a malfunctioning furnace can be dangerous, potentially leading to significant damage and higher energy bills. Signs that your furnace may need repair include:

  • A strong fuel odor coming from the unit
  • Difficulty starting the furnace or keeping it running
  • A pilot light that burns yellow instead of blue
  • Insufficient warm air from your ducts
  • Unusual noises like whistling or squealing
  • Poor indoor air quality, causing skin irritation or allergies

If you notice any of these signs, contact Carrasco AC Repairs to schedule a heating repair. We’ll promptly evaluate your furnace and recommend the best course of action.
